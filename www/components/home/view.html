Medinet Mobile Billing allows you to track both patient interactions (claims) and non patient activity. So you can track your non billable time, CME credits, etc. at the time you are performing the activities. You can opt to track your time on the fly or after the fact.

The menus are always the bottom of the screen.

Click on Claim to record a patient interaction and Activity for your non billable time.

Once you have started tracking your claims, they will show up here until you complete them and send them out for billing. Activities for last 2 weeks will always show up here.
